Our Product



The Good Morning™ band:

The Problem

There are two types of people: those who curse their alarm clocks in the morning, and those who don’t. Which category are you in right now?

That’s what we thought.

Billions of people are waking up each morning, and almost all of them are doing it begrudgingly.

The problem is that they are waking up all wrong.

Not all sleep is created equal. Your sleep happens in stages and these stages come in cycles. Waking up during the REM sleep will doubtlessly leave you feeling disoriented and exhausted.

So how can you know when you’re not in REM sleep, and thus can wake up feeling refreshed?


Other's Solutions

While the monster (fatigue) ravages on, others have tried woefully to keep it at bay. These efforts have taken the shape of audio recording devices and apps that “predict” your sleep stages and wake you up when you “sound” like you’re not in REM sleep...

...The problem?

A lot of different things can affect your breathing while you sleep. Ever tried to sleep with a head cold?

To get an accurate read on your sleep stages, you need to get a little deeper than the sound of your breathing.

Our Solution

Good Morning™ is the only programmable wellness product currently on the market that tracks actual physiological parameters that can accurately monitor your sleep cycle.

Thus it is able to wake you up within a half hour of your scheduled alarm time at the moment when you will wake feeling most rested.

Simply set your desired wake time, and sleep easy.

Keep the Good Morning™ band on throughout the day with its attractive design and simple watch functions, or leave it by your bedside for your next night’s sleep.



Metrics



Contact with the body enables the following physiological parameters to be measured in order to accurately track your sleep cycle:
  • Heart rate
  • Sleep cycle
  • Body movement
  • Blood pressure/flow
  • Body temperature
  • Brain activity
  • Eye movement
  • Breathing
